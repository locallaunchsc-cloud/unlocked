# Privacy Policy for UNLOCKED

**Last Updated: March 9, 2026**

## Introduction

UNLOCKED ("we", "our", or "the app") is a social confidence building app developed by Jay Fischer. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

## Information We Collect

UNLOCKED is designed with your privacy in mind. The app operates entirely on your device.

- **Local Data Only**: All app data including mission progress, journal entries, and settings are stored locally on your device.
- **No Account Required**: We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information.
- **No Analytics or Tracking**: We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Data Storage

All data generated through your use of the app is stored locally on your device using on-device storage. We do not transmit, collect, or store any of your data on external servers.

## Third-Party Services

UNLOCKED does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

## Children's Privacy

UNLOCKED is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
